<h1>CRAZY COLLISIONS</h1>

This is not a game about the chaos.

Crazy Collisions is a fast-paced roguelike where every click unleashes unpredictable madness. Spawn balls, watch them crash, bounce, multiply, and completely spiral out of control.

Your mission sounds simple:
complete the objective before time runs out.

But here's the catch...
luck is always part of the equation.

Each round you survive brings you to a shop with 3 random items. Some will turn you into an unstoppable force. Others might ruin everything in seconds.

You'll never get the same run twice.
You'll never feel fully in control.
And that's exactly the point.

Adapt. Improvise. Survive the chaos.

<h3>What to expect</h3> - Physics-driven insanity - Roguelike progression with unpredictable builds - Quick rounds, constant tension - Simple to play… hard to master

One run you're a genius.
The next one? Total disaster.

So… how far can you really go?
TRY YOUR LUCK. PUSH YOUR SKILL. EMBRACE THE COLLISIONS.
